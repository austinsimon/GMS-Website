JOIN

Where is it?

Located in the MAE-C building on Gale Lemerand Drive, across from Gator Dining and East Hall. You can find the entrance to our shop next to the Gator Motorsports sign.

Do I need any experience?

No! We teach you everything in the shop. Because our car is so customized, even people with prior knowledge of cars and motorsports can learn new things.

How do I join?

Come into the shop any time after 6:00 on weekdays, and noon on the weekends. We are located right across the street from Gator Dining, so you can stop by on the way back from dinner. You can also email us at recruitment@gatormotorsports.com.

What do I do when I get to the shop?

Ask questions! There will always be someone at the shop working on the car, and they will be happy to answer any questions. Coming to the shop and asking questions when you can is how you become a member of GMS, so don’t be shy!
